What's free, what's paid

Last updated: 16 May 2026

We're putting this on the record so there are no surprises later. Here's what Amble costs today, what's planned, and our promise to you.

Today

Amble is free. Every feature in the app is free. No ads. No tracking. No upsells while you're walking.

  • Generate fresh walking routes from your door
  • Save and rate your routes
  • Track walks with live GPS, ETA, and pace
  • Dog Mode with multi-dog profiles
  • Walk history with photos and replay
  • Pass-by picks, Pocket Mode, Head Back Now, mid-walk detours
  • Elevation, lit-paths-after-sunset, beach and park biasing

Coming later — Amble Pro

We're building an optional Pro tier for people who walk a lot. Pro adds new features on top of the free app — it doesn't take anything away.

Here's what's coming, and why each one is in:

☁️ Cloud sync across devices
Your walks, saved routes, dog profiles and photos back up automatically. Switch to a new phone, sign in, everything's there. Today, if you lose your phone, you lose your walking history. Pro fixes that.
📚 Unlimited walk history
Free users keep their most recent 50 walks — about three months for a daily walker. Pro keeps every walk you've ever recorded. Worth it if you want to look back at "what did I walk this time last year?"
🌧 Weather-aware route biasing
Free Amble warns you when rain's coming. Pro Amble actually routes around it — biases toward sheltered streets, woodland, or covered paths when wet, and toward shade and water when it's hot.
⌚ Apple Watch + Live Activities
Glance at remaining distance, pace, and next turn on your wrist. Leave the phone in your pocket — the whole point of Pocket Mode, taken to the logical conclusion. Includes Live Activity on the iPhone lock screen.
🥾 Multi-day trail planner
For people walking long-distance routes — the Coast Path, the West Highland Way, your own week-long loop. Plot daily stages, mark overnight stops, see the whole route across days. Niche, but the niche actually walks.
👨‍👩‍👧 Family sharing
One subscription covers up to six members of your household. If two of you walk, two of you get Pro.
📤 Bulk GPX export
Single-walk export stays free for everyone — that's the share-with-a-friend case. Pro adds "export everything" — your full history as one file, useful if you want to move data to another tool or back it up yourself.

Pricing (not final)

The plan is around £2.99/month or £24.99/year (about 30% off monthly), with a one-time £49.99 lifetime option for people who'd rather pay once and never think about it again. We'll publish the exact numbers — and confirm they haven't changed — before anyone is charged.

When?

Not soon. We're shipping the free app first, finding out what people actually use, and only then will we build Pro and turn it on. Best estimate: mid-to-late 2026. We'd rather launch Pro when it's genuinely worth paying for than rush to charge.

If you want to be told when Pro launches, email pro@amble.fit and we'll keep a list. No marketing emails — just one message when we flip the switch.

Our promise

Three rules we're holding ourselves to:

  1. Anything free today stays free, forever. Pro is for new features we build later, not features we've taken back. The route planner, walk tracker, Dog Mode, history, Pocket Mode — all of these stay free.
  2. No surprise charges. If we add a free trial, it'll be opt-in with a clear card and a real "no, don't charge me" option. No dark patterns.
  3. Cancel in one tap. If you ever do subscribe, cancelling will use Apple's or Google's standard subscription cancel — the same flow you already know.

Why we're telling you this now

We've seen the negative reviews for other walking apps. The pattern is always the same: a free feature gets moved behind a paywall and people feel cheated. Komoot, Gaia GPS, AllTrails, Strava — all of them are dealing with backlash from users who feel the rug was pulled. We don't want to be that.

Writing the promise down before there's pressure to break it is the whole point. If you ever feel we've broken it, email hello@amble.fit.
